About Us

Property Experts
You Can Rely On

Salley Property Services has been managing rental properties across Leinster for over two decades. Led by Director Michael Salley, we provide landlords with a complete, hassle-free property management service.

Whether you own a single rental or a full portfolio, we save you time, reduce costs and take the stress out of managing your investment. Our local knowledge of Dublin, Wicklow and Wexford means we find quality tenants faster.
